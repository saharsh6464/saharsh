Mysore Palace: Capturing Regal Beauty

Photography Process

This photograph of Mysore Palace was taken on a cloudy day, which added a dramatic effect to the image. The natural lighting from the overcast sky provided a soft, diffused light that helped bring out the details in the architecture while keeping the colors vibrant.

Framing and Composition

The palace was shot from a slightly lower angle, emphasizing its grandeur and scale. The composition was carefully planned to include the expansive foreground, allowing the viewer's eyes to travel from the lush greenery up to the intricate details of the palace itself.

Editing Adjustments

Basic editing was done to enhance the overall mood of the image. Adjustments were made to the contrast and saturation to enrich the colors of the palace and the surrounding sky. The image was also sharpened slightly to bring out the fine details in the architecture, making the structure stand out against the brooding sky.
